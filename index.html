Frequently Asked Questions

Are my photos safe and private?

Yes. We handle every photo with care and do all work locally. Your images are never shared with anyone outside our team.

How long does it take?

Most projects are completed within 7–10 business days, depending on the number of photos. We’ll give you a timeframe with your quote.

Can you fix damaged photos?

Light improvement is included with all scans. More advanced restoration and retouching services will be available in a future upgrade.

Do you work with slides or negatives?

We can discuss slides and negatives during your consultation and include them in a custom quote if needed.

What if I can’t leave home?

Where available, we offer home pickup for larger collections. We can also guide you through packing and safe shipping from home.

How do I access my files after?

You’ll receive your photos on a USB drive and/or in a secure online folder. We’ll walk you through how to view and share them step by step.
